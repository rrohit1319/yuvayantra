How to make your home a smart home

A smart home is a residence that uses internet-connected devices to enable automation and control of household tasks. To make your home a smart home, you can install smart devices such as smart lights, smart thermostats, smart locks, and smart speakers. You can also use a smart home assistant like Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant to control these devices with your voice. Additionally, you can use a smart home app on your phone to remotely monitor and control your home.
